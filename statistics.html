    • Poor diet and nutrition are recognised as major contributory risk factors for ill health and premature death

    26% of UK adults are obese according to recent government reports, below stats shows obesity figures in the UK;

    • 58% of women were obese or overweight

    • 68% of men were obese or overweight

    • 1 in 3 children in year 6 were obese or overweight .

    • 617,000 admissions to the NHS where obesity was recorded as factor in 2017 and up 18% on 2015/16

    Diabetes – since 1996 the number of people diagnosed with diabetes has increased from 1.4 million to 2.9 million. 2025 it’s estimated that five million people will have diabetes. Most of these cases will be type 2 diabetes because of our ageing population and rapidly rising numbers of overweight and obese people due to the lack of healthy diet and exercising.
