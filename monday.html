    Affordable meals

    7 days of healthy Nutritious meals to serve 1-4 people

    MONDAY

    Sweetcorn egg-fried rice with chicken, cost per serving - 40p

    Make use of leftovers

    You can utilise Sunday’s leftovers roast chicken and create this little effort quick meal. Add veggies such as sweetcorn, add curry, soy sauce and ketchup at the end of the cooking. Chicken is a great source of the amino acid, tryptophan. Eating chicken in the evening would aid in raising serotonin levels and help promote a good night’s sleep.

    By adding egg to this meal it boosts its protein contribution and it also provides vitamin D.

    Recipe
    sunflower oil 1 tbsp
    beaten with some seasoning 3 eggs
    mixed stir fry vegetable 320g pack
    mild curry powder 1 tbsp
    frozen sweetcorn 140g
    cooked rice 600g
    roasted chicken breast, finely shredded 1
    low salt soy sauce 2 tbsp
    sweet chilli sauce 2 tbsp
    ketchup 2 tbsp
    Cooking method

    1. Heat a splash of oil in a large frying pan and tip in the beaten eggs. Swirl the pan to coat in a thin layer of egg and cook for a few mins until set. Tip onto a chopping board, roll up, slice thinly and set aside.

    2. Heat a little more oil, add the stir-fry veg, curry powder and sweetcorn with a splash of water. Cook for 1-2 mins until the veg starts to wilt, then tip into a bowl. Add the last of the oil to the pan, tip in the rice and chicken, mix well, then add the soy sauce, sweet chilli, ketchup, a splash of water and some black pepper.

    3. Finally, add the eggs and the veg, toss together and heat through until hot. Tip into bowls and serve immediately.
